BEEF SALAD


Asian Noodle & Beef Salad is a delightful and flavorful dish that combines the richness of tender beef strips with the freshness of crisp vegetables and the satisfying texture of Asian noodles. The dish typically features marinated and seared beef, colorful veggies such as bell peppers, carrots, and cucumbers, all tossed together with cooked noodles like soba or rice noodles. The ensemble is then generously coated in a zesty and aromatic dressing made with ingredients like soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and sesame oil.

Ingredients


  • 1/4 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh gingerroot
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 beef top sirloin steak (1 pound)
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 ounces thin rice noodles
  • 1 cup julienned zucchini
