Bacon, Egg and Cheese


Any New Yorker will tell you a good bacon, egg and cheese (BEC) sandwich is the best way to start your day, and most likely you already have everything you need in your pantry to whip one up. The key elements to a great BEC are the melted cheese, scrambled egg that nestles perfectly onto the bread without falling off and salty crispy bacon.

Ingredients


  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 2 slices sharp Cheddar cheese, about 1 ounce /li>
  • 2 slices cooked bacon
  • 2 slices toasted white bread or 1 kaiser roll, split
